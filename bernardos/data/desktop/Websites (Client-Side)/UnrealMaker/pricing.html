Pay royalties only if your game success

You will need to pay royalties if your game success. If the game you made using our game engine is not success, it is not nice for us to charge you any money, right?

5% for each game that made $5000 USD or more in profit. So that mean if you have five games making $5000 USD each, you only need to pay us $1000 USD. However, if you have five games making only $4000 each, you do not need to pay us at all!
